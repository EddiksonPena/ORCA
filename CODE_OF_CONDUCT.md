# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Orca to be a welcoming, respectful project for builders, operators, researchers, and newcomers.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent while giving honest feedback
- avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- discriminatory language or conduct
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- repeated bad-faith disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community or the project.
